Decentra-Vote

Decentra-Vote

is a decentralized voting application that provides a transparent, easy, and secure platform for members of the DAO to vote and decide what funds and capital from venture capitalists are used for,to enable the smooth running of the community.


Proposals

Capital Expenditure


Capital expenditures (CAPEX) involve significant investments in long-term assets that are essential 's operations. Voting on capital expenditure proposal enables members to make decisions regarding allocation of resources for essential  activities such as purchase or upgrade equipments, infrastructure, machinery which has a long term impact on the business.

Technological Advancements


Technological advancements allows us become cost efficient and effective, this will also provide a seamless experience in  handling day to day tasks allowing us save time and cost. Voting for technology equipment proposal enables members to make  decisions regarding the application of resources which will significantly impact our workflow and allow us better compete with  established companies.

Human Resources


Human resources takes care of finding, recruiting, screening and training job applicants. Voting for Human  Resource proposal enables members to make decisions regarding the application of resources towards human  resources to establish and improve the organization's workforce which will provide the company a long term  benefit in terms of workforce in return allows us compete with already established companies.

Research Projects


With Research Projects, we aim to foster innovation and explore new frontiers. Members can vote on proposals  that focus on groundbreaking research initiatives, seeking to push the boundaries of knowledge in our industry.  By supporting Research Projects, we invest in the future and nurture a culture of discovery within our  organization.

Market Research


Market Research gives us a demographic information about the market of our product for gaining customers. Conducting market research helps us understand the demand, market size E.T.C of our product. Voting for Market  Research proposal enables members to make decisions regarding the allocation of resources for conduction of market  research which impacts our understanding of consumer behavior and improves our business idea.

Operational Expenses

Operational Expenses play a critical role in the day-to-day functioning of our organization. Voting on Operational Expenses proposals enables members to make decisions regarding the allocation of resources for essential activities such as infrastructure, staffing, and logistical needs. These decisions directly impact our operational efficiency and sustainability.
